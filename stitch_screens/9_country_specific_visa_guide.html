Commonly Rejected Reasons

warning

Incomplete Documentation

Missing original signatures or providing photocopies when originals are required is the #1 cause for delay or rejection.

account_balance_wallet

Insufficient Financial Proof

Failing to show consistent bank statements or a stable income that supports the planned itinerary and accommodation costs.

history_edu

Vague Travel Itinerary

Non-specific hotel bookings or inconsistent travel dates that don't match the visa duration requested.

lightbulb

Expert Tips for Success

Visit Japan Web Registration

Pre-register your immigration and customs information on the official 'Visit Japan Web' site to skip long manual queues at the airport.

The 'Hanko' Tradition

For business visas, ensuring all Japanese sponsor documents have the official company seal (Inkan) is crucial for authenticity.
